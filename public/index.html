The test

Okay — show us!

We have 3 games. In each game, you talk in a different way. Same job, but harder!

Rules

How to play

  • Two teams: Boys and Girls.
  • Each game, your team picks one person to do the job.
  • The team helps — but only the way the game allows.
  • Win the game = 1 point.

Game 1

No Words

How to play

One person gets a secret task — like a push-up, or touch something in the room. Make your team do it. But no talking!

  • ✋ Hands flat on the table. No pointing.
  • 😐 Only your face and sounds (like "hmm" or "ahh").
  • ⏱️ Be fast. Correct = 1 point.
1:00remaining
Game 2

Heads Up

How to play

One person looks at the word. Give clues so the team says the word. But never say the word!

  • 🗣️ You can talk. Give clues. Explain it.
  • 🚫 Don't say the word. Don't spell it.
  • ➡️ Team says it? Tap Correct. Too hard? Tap Skip.
  • ⏱️ Most correct wins.
